I am Jingnan Zheng, a PhD student of [NExT++ Research Center](https://www.nextcenter.org/) under the supervision of [Prof. Chua Tat-Seng](https://www.chuatatseng.com/) at National University of Singapore (NUS). I obtained my bachelor's degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and the University of Michigan. My current research interests focus on analyzing and mitigating vulnerabilities in foundation models, enhancing their robustness to improve safety and security.
